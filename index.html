11 — Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Still have a question after reading through? Reach out on Telegram or X.

01What platform does nukrax run on?
All Expert Advisors are built natively for MetaTrader 5, with no bridging or third-party wrappers required.
02Do I need coding experience to use an EA?
No. Each system ships with sensible defaults and a clear setup guide — configuration is handled through simple input parameters.
03Which instruments are supported?
Coverage spans major and minor forex pairs, gold, and select indices, depending on the specific Expert Advisor.
04How is risk managed per trade?
Position sizing is calculated dynamically from account equity and a configurable risk-per-trade percentage, with hard exposure caps enforced automatically.
05Can I run multiple EAs on the same account?
Yes. Each EA tracks its own risk and position state, so multiple systems can operate side by side without conflict.
06What brokers are compatible?
Any MT5 broker offering standard order execution is compatible. Low-latency ECN brokers are recommended for HFT-class systems.
07Do you offer backtest results?
Each Expert Advisor page includes backtest methodology notes. Full historical reports are available on request.
08Is a VPS required?
A VPS is strongly recommended for continuous uptime, especially for latency-sensitive HFT strategies.
09How often are strategies updated?
Systems are reviewed on a rolling basis as market conditions shift, with updates released as needed rather than on a fixed schedule.
10What is the minimum account size?
Minimums vary by EA and are listed on each system's individual page, based on its position sizing model.
11Do the EAs trade news events?
Some systems include dedicated news-based logic; others include filters to reduce exposure around high-impact releases. This is configurable per EA.
12Can I customize the risk settings?
Yes. Risk-per-trade, maximum exposure, and session filters are all exposed as adjustable inputs.
13Is live support available?
Direct support is available through Telegram, with community discussion and updates posted on X.
14How does nukrax.tr differ from nukrax.cr?
Both are live trading platforms with journaling and analysis tools — they serve as parallel environments integrated with the EA suite.
15Will more Expert Advisors be released?
Yes — additional systems are in active development and testing, as noted in the Execution Systems section above.
